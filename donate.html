Who We Are

The Sanctuary provides the elephants with protected habitats, quality meals, and automatic waterers. Our Husbandary and Vet Teams take care of the elephants in all areas and monitor them via cameras as their habitats continue to grow. We promote educational programs to raise awareness about the importance of conservation. Your generosity enables us to meet the needs of elephants for the next 30 years and beyond!
